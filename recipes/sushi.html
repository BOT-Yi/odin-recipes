Sushi

Description

This recipe is for two people using four sheets of nori. You would each make two rools of hosomaki then one roll of futomakito total of 15 pieces each. In two hours you could make twice as much from start to finish.

Multiply this recipe for the number of people you have or the amount of sushi rolls you want to make.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Water
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Wasabi Powder
  • Nori
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Tuna
